Remote Architectural Intern / Designer

Compensation: $50,000 annually

Our ideal associate must have a passion for design, be a versatile professional and have an entrepreneurial spirit. This is a growth opportunity working on a range of commercial multifamily projects, student housing, hospitality and assisted living facilities.

InvestWell Architects are talented professionals that understand the ultimate goals of our clients. We live by our mission of "Designs for Life". We strive for excellence in producing quality architectural services and built environments that are impactful, meaningful, and truly worth our clients investment. We are successful because we encourage an environment where everyone has a voice, opinion, vision and an opportunity to contribute.

  • Eligible to work in the United States of America

  • Ability to manage time and meet deadlines

Salary is commensurate upon experience.
